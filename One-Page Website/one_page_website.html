Buy - Sell - Trade - Learn

Thanks for visiting!

We are here to help with all your music needs.
We are a music club that helps facilitate the exchange of guitars and stringed instruments,
and all other things musical, from instruments and equipment, to lessons and musical knowledge.
We also serve as a hub for announcing and tracking live music events
in the vibrant Tampa Bay live music scene, one of the best in the world.

Please submit the short form in our CONTACT section to be notified of new live events.
If you are new to the guitar, enjoy some "getting started" videos in ourVIDEOS section.
